Connecting Coastlines & Experiences

The Digital Platform for Authentic African Coastal Tourism.

**Our Vision:** To become the leading international platform connecting travelers to accessible, safe, and locally-operated sea vessels across Africa and beyond within 3 years.

The Fragmented Coastal Opportunity

The vast coastal tourism market in Kenya and Africa is missing its most authentic layer. This disconnect creates significant problems for both tourists and local operators, representing a massive, untapped market.

No Dedicated Platform

No centralized booking for small boat operators (fishing, snorkeling, sightseeing). Tourists face limited choices and a high reliance on offline agents.

Luxury-Only Focus

Existing digital services cater only to large cruise ships or high-end yacht charters, leaving small operators with zero digital visibility.

Unverified Safety

Booking often happens on the beach with no guaranteed safety standards, eroding customer trust and creating an unstable, low-quality experience.

Scams & Fraud

Tourists risk scams and fraud, while operators are forced to rely on low-margin agent bookings. Our focus is on trust, integrity, and safety.

Business Model & Growth
